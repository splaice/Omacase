## Omarchy

Omacase is inspired by Omarchy and downloads Omarchy theme metadata and
wallpapers at runtime for the Omarchy-derived themes listed in `themes/manifest`.

Omarchy is copyright David Heinemeier Hansson and released under the MIT License.

https://github.com/basecamp/omarchy

Downloaded Omarchy artifacts are cached under `~/.local/share/omacase/upstream/`
and `~/.local/share/omacase/backgrounds/`; generated local app fragments are
written under `~/.local/share/omacase/generated/themes/`.
